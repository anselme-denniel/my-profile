2 times world champion

Verstappen is a Dutch Formula 1 driver. He races with the RedBull team with teammate Sergio Perez. He is twice world champion, last year and this year even before the season is over.

RedBull racing

Biographie

Max Verstappen, aka the Lion, was born on September 30, 1997 and is the son of pilot Jos Verstappen. He started at RedBull's secondary team, ToroRosso, then transferred to RedBull.

An extraordinary rivalry

Last year Max Verstappen raced all season against seven-time world champion Lewis Hamilton. Until the last race they were tied and Max emerged victorious.
